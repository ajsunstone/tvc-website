Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about eligibility, interest calculation, security, and more.

Who is eligible to apply?

Individuals with valid PAN and verifiable monthly income are eligible to apply. Final approval depends on underwriting and KYC verification.

How is interest calculated?

Interest is charged only on the amount you utilize and for the exact number of days until repayment. No usage, no interest.

What documents are required?

Basic KYC documents including PAN, proof of identity and address, and income proof as per product type.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-grade encryption and follow regulatory guidelines and best practices to keep your information secure.

How quickly can I get funds?

For on-demand credit, approved withdrawals are usually disbursed within minutes during banking hours.
